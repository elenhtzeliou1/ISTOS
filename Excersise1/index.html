MAIN INTRO

Welcome to InfoHub — an Informatics e-learning space built to support your studies with clear explanations, practical examples, and organized learning paths. Instead of jumping between scattered notes, random links, and long video searches, you get one connected platform where courses, book lists, and video playlists work together. Explore topics like programming, networks, databases, web development, AI, and cybersecurity at your own pace, revisit difficult concepts whenever you need, and follow structured content that helps you progress step by step. Our goal is simple: to make Informatics feel more understandable, more practical, and more useful for both your academic journey and your future career.

01

OUR COURSES

Our courses are designed to be focused, student-friendly, and truly useful — each one broken into clear modules that guide you from fundamentals to confident understanding. You’ll learn through short explanations, real examples, and practical tasks that help you apply concepts immediately, whether you’re writing code, designing a database, understanding network protocols, building web apps, exploring AI ideas, or strengthening your cybersecurity skills. Every course is supported by carefully selected books and video playlists, so you can revise faster, go deeper when needed, and build a study routine that fits your pace and goals.

02

BOOKS & VIDEO LISTS

Our book and video lists are curated to help you study smarter, not longer. Instead of endless searching and guessing which sources are worth your time, you’ll find carefully selected textbooks, guides, and high-quality video playlists that match each topic and difficulty level — from beginner-friendly introductions to deeper, more advanced references. Use these recommendations to reinforce what you learn in courses, prepare for exams, review key concepts quickly, or explore new areas like cybersecurity, database design, web development, and AI with resources that are clear, trusted, and relevant to Informatics students.

03

OUR TEAM

Behind this platform is a two-person team of fourth-year Informatics university students who built InfoHub as part of a course assignment, with the goal of creating something genuinely useful for fellow students. We designed the site around real study needs we face ourselves — clear structure, reliable resources, and easy navigation between courses, books, and video materials. While the project began as an academic task, we treated it like a real learning product: organized by topic, focused on practical skills, and built to support studying, revision, and steady progress.

04

Explore Our Categories

Our platform is organized into six core Informatics areas: Programming, Web Development, Databases, Networks, Cybersecurity, and Artificial Intelligence. Each category brings together everything you need in one place—structured lessons, recommended resources, and connected content—so you can study without jumping between scattered materials. Whether you’re building strong foundations or exploring more advanced topics, you can choose a category that matches your goals, follow a clear progression, and move confidently from concept to real understanding.
