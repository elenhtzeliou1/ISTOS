A few words about our team

We are a two-person team of fourth-year Informatics university students, and we built InfoHub as part of the course “Technologies and Programming of Applications on the Web.” Our goal was to create a clean, organized e-learning space that brings together courses, book lists, and video resources in key Informatics areas, so students can study more effectively without jumping between scattered materials.

InfoHub was developed as part of the first assignment for the course “Technologies and Programming of Applications on the Web.”

Our platform was designed to make studying simpler and more organized. Instead of searching through scattered sources, you can find courses, recommended books, and selected videos in one place—grouped by category and difficulty level. Each section includes clear descriptions and key details, so you know what you will learn before you start.

Our goal is to provide an environment that genuinely supports Informatics students: fast navigation, easy filtering, curated recommendations, and clean presentation of resources. This way, you can follow a steady study plan, revise effectively for exams, and build your knowledge step by step.
